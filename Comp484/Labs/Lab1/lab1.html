??/??/1997: The Good Old Days!

Diablo. Released Dec. 31, 1996. Proved to be a major hit in the video gaming industry. My brother and I probably dedicated hundreds of hours fighting our way through dungeons and upgrading our gear. I briefly played Diablo 2 some time after it came out. My assessment: You can't beat the original.

02/12/2015: This Game Never Gets Old

Lands of Lore. Released in 1993. Some claim that this was the greatest RPG made for its time and continues to be one of the best ever made. I've played through this game and its sequel more times than I can remember. At this very moment I have a "Let's Play Lands of Lore" video running in the background. I now days enjoy using my CS knowledge to hack and manipulate the game.
